About me

I am a skilled iOS developer with strong proficiency in Swift, SwiftUI, and Objective-C, complemented by extensive 7+ years experience in various iOS frameworks, including Apple Watch development. My commitment to staying abreast of the latest trends in iOS development drives my passion for crafting exceptional user experiences and optimizing application performance. Recently, I have also begun exploring cross-platform development using Flutter and React Native, aiming to expand my skill set and create seamless applications for both iOS and Android platforms.

Throughout my career, I have successfully delivered innovative and user-friendly mobile applications that not only meet but exceed user expectations.
